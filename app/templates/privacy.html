Effective Date: July 21, 2026 Last Updated: July 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Anthony Magno ("eTrackMo," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, protects, and processes personal information through the eTrackMo website, application, platform, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

eTrackMo is currently operated as an independent software project by Anthony Magno in the Philippines.

This Privacy Policy is intended to provide clear information about our personal data processing practices and our commitment to protecting personal information in accordance with applicable Philippine privacy laws, including Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

2. Personal Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Full name or display name.
  • Email address.
  • User account identifier.
  • Authentication and account security information handled through our authentication infrastructure.

Financial Information

If you choose to use financial management features, you may provide information relating to your personal finances, including account names, balances, transactions, categories, notes, and other information you choose to record.

You control the financial information that you choose to enter into the Service.

Sharing Information

If you use sharing features, we may process information regarding the people, groups, permissions, and financial information that you choose to share.

Technical Information

We may collect technical information necessary to operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Service. This may include information such as browser type, device information, operating system information, IP address, authentication events, and technical logs.

3. How We Use Personal Information

We may process personal information for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing user accounts.
  • Authenticating users and maintaining account security.
  • Providing and operating the Service.
  • Storing and displaying information that users choose to manage through the Service.
  • Enabling user-controlled sharing features.
  • Communicating with users regarding account, security, service, or support matters.
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical problems.
  • Improving the performance, reliability, and functionality of the Service.
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases and Privacy Principles

Personal information is processed only for declared, specified, and legitimate purposes and in a manner consistent with applicable law.

Depending on the circumstances, processing may be based on your consent, the performance of a contract or provision of a service requested by you, compliance with a legal obligation, protection of vital interests, or another lawful basis recognized by applicable law.

We seek to follow the principles of transparency, legitimate purpose, and proportionality in our processing of personal information.

5. Financial Information and Sensitive Information

Financial information may be sensitive in nature and should be treated carefully.

You should only provide information that is necessary for the features you choose to use.

You should not submit passwords, PINs, one-time passwords, authentication codes, or other credentials for your bank, e-wallet, or financial accounts unless the Service explicitly provides a secure feature designed to receive such information.

eTrackMo does not request or require your banking passwords or authentication credentials for the basic personal finance management features of the Service.

We may share personal information in the following circumstances:

At Your Direction

Information may be shared with people or groups when you intentionally use the Service to grant them access or visibility.

We may use third-party service providers that help us operate the Service, such as providers of authentication, cloud infrastructure, hosting, storage, security, or other technical services.

Such providers may process personal information only as necessary to provide their services to us and subject to appropriate contractual or organizational safeguards where applicable.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal information when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, legal process, lawful government requests, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, eTrackMo, or others.

7. Third-Party Technology Providers

eTrackMo may use third-party infrastructure and technology providers to operate the Service.

These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and applicable agreements.

We seek to use reputable providers and configure services with appropriate security controls for the intended processing activities.
